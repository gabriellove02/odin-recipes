Pasta salad

"A homemade Italian pasta salad dressing takes this simple pasta salad loaded with veggies, cheese, pepperoni, and olives to a whole new level! Serve it right away or make it ahead for packed lunches, picnics, or summer barbecues." - BONNIES

Link to original page on allrecipes.com

Quick facts

  • Prep time: 30 mins
  • Cook time: 15 mins
  • Total time: 45 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pepperoni
  • Provolone cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Red onion
  • Cucumber
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 8-10 minutes
  2. Add the ingredients and pasta in a big bowl
  3. Let chill for about 8 hours in the fridge
  4. Serve!
